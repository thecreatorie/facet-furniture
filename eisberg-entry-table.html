About this piece

When creating the Eisberg Entry Table we were inspired by icebergs, because their massive size is almost completely hidden by the dark water they reside in. Three reclaimed oak legs hold up a tabletop finished in our high gloss paint. Sure to make a statement in any room — perfect for entryways, hallways, behind a sofa, or in tight corners.
