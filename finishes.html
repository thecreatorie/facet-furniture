About Living Finishes

Our Living finishes are natural hand-applied finishes that will change over time more than any of our other finishes. You may feel the need to apply a natural oil rejuvenator periodically (typically once or twice a year). This product can be purchased directly from us. This natural evolution is by design — your piece develops character unique to your home.
