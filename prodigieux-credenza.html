About this piece

The Prodigieux Credenza is an eye catcher — built from walnut or white oak and sitting atop modern bronze feet. This marble topped credenza features 3 compartments that you can design to be cabinets with push latches or drawers. Exceptionally versatile storage for bar, dining, or living spaces.
