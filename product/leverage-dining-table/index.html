About this piece

The Leverage Dining Table is a modern-day marvel, guaranteed to captivate and retain everyone's attention. Bold in form and sleek in style, this table offers not only ample surface area but also unlimited chair placement options with its solid center base. Hidden fasteners and a touch of burnished brass add an extra layer of sophistication.

Apart from the natural Ghost Cerused White Oak finish, the Leverage Dining Table is available in walnut with a wide range of unique finishes. With three sizing options, it easily accommodates most dining rooms and even spacious kitchens. The generous surface and slim frame allow this table to function as both a dining table and a versatile work-from-home space.
