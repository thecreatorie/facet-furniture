About this piece

Edgy and elegant, the Motley Coffee Table boasts a unique fusion of textures and patterns, creating a striking asymmetrical design. The thick white oak tabletop contrasts with the marble leg and a parchment skin waterfall, ensuring this eye-catching piece becomes the centerpiece in any room it graces.

The rustic, stone-like texture of the parchment forms a bold contrast with the modern marble leg on the opposite side. Further contrast is achieved with the darker black accents within the lighter gray parchment, compared to the bright white accents in the dark black marble.

Though artistically distinct in design, the Motley Coffee Table is available in a wide variety of finishes for the oak table, parchment, and marble leg, allowing you to make a statement with this one-of-a-kind piece.
